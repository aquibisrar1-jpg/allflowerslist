About Humulus lupulus

Common Hops, or Humulus lupulus, is a vigorous climbing perennial vine native to parts of Europe, Asia, and North America. While not typically grown for showy flowers, it produces distinctive greenish-yellow cone-like structures (strobiles) used extensively in brewing beer. It is a fast-growing plant.
