About Urtica

Urtica, commonly known as stinging nettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant known for its stinging hairs, not its flowers. Found in temperate regions worldwide, its small, greenish flowers are inconspicuous. It is cultivated for its medicinal and culinary properties.
